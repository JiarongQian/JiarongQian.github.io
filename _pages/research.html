--- ## Large-Scale Models for Medical Image Quality Control

Project Objective:

Develop a multimodal medical imaging quality control model that automatically assesses the acquisition quality of multi-site human images and supports clinical diagnosis, particularly assisting doctors with limited reading experience in resource-constrained region.

--- ## ToothFussion: Generate a completed tooth from crown information

Project Objective:

To generate complete CBCT tooth structures from partial IOS crown information and textual descriptions, thereby reducing dental patients' exposure to CT radiation and lowering the risk of radiation-induced malignancies.
